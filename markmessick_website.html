It All Started With a Simple Question...

Out of all the restaurants in NYC...

Which one are you most likely to get stabbed at?

Hi, my name is Mark Messick. I'm a data scientist in training, and recently my team and I set out to determine which restaurant in New York City is the most dangerous.

Our hypothesis? Definitely McDoanald's.

Granted, none of us were from New York. In fact, none of us had ever even been to a McDonald's in New York. But with almost no reasoning other than just a gut feeling, we all asserted that McDonald's is the most dangerous restaurant in NYC. And then, with nothing more but a single CSV with 3 years worth of NYC arrest data, we set out on a memorable journey to prove our point.

Let us show you what we found...
