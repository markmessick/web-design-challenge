Identify the Total Number of Restaurants

Despite our inital success in generating our first chart, we quickly realized that it was not a conclusive analysis of the most dangerous restaurant in NYC. While there are more violent crimes committed near McDonald's restaurants than any other competitor...there are also more McDonald's locations. So of course they have the highest number of reported violent crimes.

To provide a more accurate insight, we used the Google Maps data to generate a report of how many restaurant locations each franchise had.

As you may guess, the number of restuarant locations almost perfectly modeled the number of violent crimes reported near each franchise.
